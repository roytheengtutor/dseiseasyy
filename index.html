About Me

As a highly experienced educator with over five years of professional teaching experience and a track record of success, I believe that I possess the skills and knowledge necessary to make a valuable contribution to your learning progress as well as the improvement of grades.

HKDSE Curriculum

I specialize in teaching HKDSE English Language and Literature, and have achieved exceptional academic credentials in this field. I obtained a level 5* in the HKDSE exam, demonstrating my mastery of advanced English concepts and skills. Furthermore, obtaining 5** in paper 2 is a testament to my expertise in academic essay writing and analysis, which is a primary strength of mine as an English tutor.

IELTS Syllabus

I also specialize in teaching IELTS syllabus, having achieved exceptional results on my own language proficiency exams, scoring band 9 in both reading and listening papers in particular. By leveraging my knowledge and expertise, I am confident that I can help my students achieve similar levels of success in their own language studies.

By instilling these essential competencies, I empower my pupils to excel not just in their English studies, but in all areas of their academic and professional endeavors.
